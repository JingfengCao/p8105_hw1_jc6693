### short discription

This dataset contains variables of origin, year, month, day, hour, temp,
dewp, humid, wind_dir, wind_speed, wind_gust, precip, pressure, visib,
time_hour. And it contains 358 rows and 15 columns. The mean temperature
is 39.5821229.
